A Little About Me

I am 18 years old studying Computer Science at Robert Gordon University. My interests have always been in computers and business. Since the age of 13 I’ve been attempting to start various business ventures online from selling servers to running a graphic design company. Scottish Patter was the welcome break which showed me the world of media in which I have enjoyed every second of from working with companies such as wearewire and Tennent’s to Staeles Design and hopefully much more I’d admit I often fall head first into businesses before I know the trade, however, the steep learning curves are what I value the most in having the experiences at my age.
